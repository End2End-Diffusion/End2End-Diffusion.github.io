Visual Representation Learning

Advancing Visual Representation and Generation Through End-to-End Training

End-to-end training is the keystone of modern deep learning. However, diffusion training remains two-stage: stage 1 for representation (VAE/RAE) and stage 2 for generation (DiT/SiT/JiT). Our mission is to enable end-to-end training for both visual representation and generation: allowing both representations and generative models to be advanced together in an end-to-end manner.
